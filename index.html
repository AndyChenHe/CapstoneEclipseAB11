Problem

Information about doctors are scattered in Malaysia

The current ways of getting doctor information are mainly through word-of-mouth or searching through individual healthcare institution’s website. This creates an information gap between doctors and patients in Malaysia.

Our Solution

Users discover doctors by specialty, condition, and location

We gather all doctor information in one place and show you people’s recommended doctors

Doctors present their services online and engage new patients

About MYDoc

MYDoc is a comprehensive doctor information platform that aims at helping people in Malaysia to search for doctor information related to their unique medical needs efficiently.

We hope MYDoc will be a new way for people to recommend doctor and make doctors more discoverable for people seeking their services.
